About.



As a giver & fixer, I tend to want to always find a way to help my family & friends. And while I love to help, my passion for assisting others was a win/lose situation for me when it came to my career. Working under many titles, such as hotel sales, marketing, & event manager, I would win by bringing in clients & revenue for the company. I produced & attended events to boost brand awarenesss for the hotel but it was a loss for me because I couldn't truly help all of my clients - mainly entrpreneurs, small businesses, & nonprofits.


Knowing that they couldn't truly fulfill their destinies hurt me the most. Whether it was company guidelines or financially overwhelming numbers, there were barriers that were disheartening & discouraging for my clients & I. Not being able to assist on my own terms would weigh heavily on my mind. So throughout the years of working in diverse segments of hospitality, my passion to help others win grew into a dream of owning an event center & management company.


And voila! I created Destiny Fulfilled to be able to express my passion of helping others on my own terms & still be able to showcase my expertise to help bring balance to small businesses, entrpreneurs, & nonprofits. Everybody wins!
